Our Privacy Commitment

CoinRewards is committed to protecting your personal information. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how it's used.

1. Information We Collect

When you use CoinRewards, we collect the following categories of information:

Account Information:

  • Email address (used for account identification and communication)
  • Password (stored in encrypted form; we never have access to your plaintext password)
  • Account creation timestamp

Activity Data:

  • Articles read and completion timestamps
  • Daily login and coin claim records
  • Coin balance and transaction history
  • Redemption requests and payment details provided during redemption
  • Chat messages sent in the Global Chat feature

Technical Data:

  • IP address and general geographic region (for fraud prevention)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To manage your account, track coin balances, and process redemption requests
  • Fraud prevention: To detect and prevent abuse such as automated coin earning, multiple accounts, or fraudulent redemptions
  • Communications: To send account-related emails including verification, redemption updates, and important policy changes
  • Platform improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the platform's content and features
  • Advertising: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be shared with advertising partners to improve ad relevance. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers.

3. Advertising and Third Parties

CoinRewards is an advertising-supported platform. We work with advertising partners who may display ads on our platform. These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests.

Our advertising partners operate independently and have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through our platform.

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. Advertisers receive aggregated data (such as total impressions and clicks) but not your individual identity or activity.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a PostgreSQL-based database platform with enterprise-grade security. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorized CoinRewards systems and personnel only.

While we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for fraud prevention purposes.

Chat messages in the Global Chat are retained for up to 90 days before automatic deletion. Article reading records and coin transaction history are retained for 12 months.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

CoinRewards uses essential cookies to maintain your login session. We may also use analytics cookies to understand how users navigate the platform. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may impair platform functionality including the ability to stay logged in.

8. Children's Privacy

CoinRewards is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email. Continued use of the platform after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
